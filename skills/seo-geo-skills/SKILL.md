# SEO & GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) Skills

Comprehensive SEO and GEO toolkit for optimizing websites in traditional search engines and AI search platforms (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews).

## Philosophy

> **GEO-first, SEO-supported.** AI search is eating traditional search.
> This toolkit optimizes for where traffic is going, not where it was.
