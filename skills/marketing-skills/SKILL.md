# Marketing Skills

Comprehensive marketing toolkit covering copywriting, content strategy, email marketing, advertising, pricing, and product launches.

## When to Use

Use this skill when:
- Writing marketing copy (headlines, CTAs, landing pages)
- Developing content strategy
- Creating email sequences and campaigns
- Designing ad creatives and campaigns
- Planning product launches
- Analyzing competitors and alternatives
- Setting pricing strategy
- Creating referral/affiliate programs

## Copywriting Principles

1. **Clarity over cleverness** - If choosing, choose clear
2. **Benefits over features** - What it means for the customer
3. **Specificity over vagueness** - Concrete details win
4. **Customer language** - Mirror voice of customer
5. **One idea per section** - Build logical flow
